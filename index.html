District Implementation Timelines
By Fiscal Year Quarter
Phase 1: Planning
Q3 2025-26 (Jan–Jun 2026)
  • Grantee kickoff meeting (January 12, 2026)
  • Program coordinator onboarding and framework development
  • Initial infrastructure setup (platforms, VR hardware, software)
  • Mentor/mentee recruitment and initial matching
  • Data collection baselines established
Phase 2: Implementation
2026-27 Full Year
  • Full training program rollout across all districts
  • Mentor/mentee program in active operation
  • VR pedagogy training series (Kern CCD)
  • LIFT alliance regional meet-up meetings
  • Annual progress reporting to CCCCO
Phase 3: Evaluation
2027-28 + Q4 Close
  • Final impact studies and program evaluations
  • Outcomes data analysis and disaggregation
  • White papers and governance board resolutions (SDCCD)
  • Institutionalization of best practices
  • Final grant reporting to fund monitor
Phase 4: Sustainability
Post-Grant (2028-29+)
  • Programs institutionalized into regular district operations
  • VR hardware and software maintained by college general funds
  • LIFT platform continued by alliance colleges
  • District EEO plans updated to reflect new practices
  • Mentorship programs transitioned to permanent sub-committees
